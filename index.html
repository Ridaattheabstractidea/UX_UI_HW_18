My Work

CafeConnect

CafeConnect is an app that aims to solve the problem of remote workers struggling to find suitable workspaces that offer the amenities they need to be productive. Many remote workers find themselves wasting time and energy trying to find a cafe with reliable Wi-Fi, power outlets, and a conducive atmosphere for work. This can negatively impact their performance, causing them to lose valuable working hours and productivity.
